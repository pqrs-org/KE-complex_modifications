Scroll the timeline with mouse button 3

Mouse button 3 Hold down button 3 to scroll the timeline horizontally. This automatically selects the hand tool. When you release button 3, it goes back to the selection tool.

Faster ripple edit with the mouse

Left shift + Button 1 (left-click) Makes a cut on the timeline
Button 2 (right-click) Ripple edit to the previous cut on the timeline
