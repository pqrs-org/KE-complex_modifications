All my effort is the parody of Capslock-Enhancement (@Vonng) & MyKeymap (@xianyukang).

Extend ; or 3 keystroke more powerful for developer , Keep your fingers stay at keyboard hot section.

Author：miozus (miozus@outlook.com)

Github: miozus/CapslockMagic

 

3 Awake Digital Keyboard

Press number 3 to 3 number, and hold it to ✱ Hyper to handler number transaction.

3 + number = f1~12

3 + word = digital Keyboard

 

Semicolon Pull Down Symbol

Press ; Semicolon to ; Semicolon, and hold it to ✱ Hyper to pull down Symbol from top.

So you don't have to hold shift and find the symbol which is far way from finger

Why design like this ?

Most design comes from @xianyukang who is a vimer, developer and refractor it for many years. I am a vimer too. So I try to evovle it at those aspects below:

Element Loactor

  • H % bracket couple
  • J ; last operation, used frequently
  • K ` markdown lover
  • L " String variable, meet everyday
  • E ^ U $ focus on the start/end of statement, but it is reverse on the number at vim / Regex, which is painful for human. I saw Oasis in the desert when I met it at the first time
  • B { bracket inside
  • Y @ apply macro, such as press qq to record, two hands cooperates.

Game Experience

  • N - M + I found that item counter which 'minus left and add right' in a game, so I remained it.
  • S E D F which looks like direction in game < ^ >

Word Abbreviation

  • V | Vertical，letter V is symmetry, keystroke V almost in the keyboard center
  • T ~ Tide（; shift T mapTo 4 spaces）。
  • C . comma/dot/period
  • X _ Xia Hua Xian
  • G ! Gan Tan Hao
  • D = Deng Hao

Hot Operation

  • Z undo: it is useful when you typo or delete somthing.
  • O swith Input Method: say goodby to Control ␣
