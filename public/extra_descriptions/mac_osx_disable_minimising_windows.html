Disable Hiding and Minimising Windows

CMD + H hides the frontmost application and CMD + Option + H hides all other applications. These shortcuts are easy to hit by accident and send windows to the Dock where they are out of sight and require extra clicks to recover. Disabling them eliminates that friction and keeps your workspace predictable.

cmd + h (hide the active application) Disabled
cmd + option + h (hide all other applications) Disabled
cmd + option + m (minimise all windows) Disabled
